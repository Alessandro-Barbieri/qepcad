QEPCAD Example: The X-Axis Ellipse Problem

The X-Axis Ellipse Problem is a typical benchmark for quantifier elimination algorithms. It asks for a characterization of all ellipses of the form 
(x - c)^2      y^2
--------   +  -----  - 1
  a^2          b^2
(i.e. ellipses centered on the x-axis) that are contained in the unit disc. In words, for all (x,y) on the ellipse, (x,y) is in the unit disc.
