Proving A nowhere identically zero

A = 4 a b d^2 - 5 b d^2 - 5 a d^2 + 6 d^2 + 4 a b c^2 - 5 b c^2
    - 5 a c^2 + 6 c^2 - 10 a b c + 12 b c + 12 a c - 14 c + 6 a b - 7 b
    - 7 a + 8
[ 
Show that A does not vanish identically anywhere
returns FALSE, 10 milliseconds reported
]
(c,d,a)
3
(E c)(E d)(E a)
[
d > 0
/\
4 d^2 a + 4 c^2 a - 10 c a + 6 a - 5 d^2 - 5 c^2 + 12 c - 7 = 0 <-- this becomes A_3,1
/\
-5 d^2 a - 5 c^2 a + 12 c a - 7 a + 6 d^2 + 6 c^2 - 14 c + 8 = 0
].
prop
go
eqn-const-l (A_3,1)
eqn-const-l (A_3,2)
st 1
rem-pj 2,2 <-- This is only justified if A_3,1 never vanishes identically
y
go
fast
go
sol T

/\
d > 0
].
prop
