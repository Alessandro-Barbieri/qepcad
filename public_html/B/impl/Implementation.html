Extension's to QEPCAD
The major extensions to QEPCAD are:
  • sfext - These are all the solution formula construction and CAD simplification extentsions.
  • adj2d - Extensions for computing adjacency information in 2D CADs, including the ability to perform some topologically defined assignments of truth values - e.g. computing closures of sets.
  • rend - Extensions for producing plots of 2D CADs.
